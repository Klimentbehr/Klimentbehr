Promotional Event

A projected business-to-business campaign designed to convert technically qualified mission planners into Rocket Lab sales opportunities.

Rocket Lab USA should launch a projected Mission Ready Challenge for organizations planning small-satellite, research, defense, and public-sector missions. Qualified participants would attend a technical webinar, submit a short mission concept, and receive a launch-readiness assessment. The campaign supports Rocket Lab’s integrated offering of launch, spacecraft, components, and mission services (Rocket Lab USA, 2026).

The projected funnel begins with 1,200 targeted contacts and produces 400 registrations, 60 qualified leads, 12 proposal requests, and approximately two contracts.

The estimated campaign cost is $185,000. The modeled economics support the campaign because a limited number of high-value business contracts can cover the promotional investment. The projection is an academic scenario and is not Rocket Lab guidance.
