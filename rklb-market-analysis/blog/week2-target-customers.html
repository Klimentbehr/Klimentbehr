Target Customers

Four customer segments provide a structured pool for campaign targeting, representation analysis, and cost justification.

The target pool includes university research laboratories, satellite startups, aerospace and defense firms, and public-sector mission teams. Research laboratories would be reached through scientific conferences and partnerships. Startups would be reached through accelerators and targeted media. Aerospace and defense firms require account-based outreach, while public-sector teams require procurement and technical briefings.
